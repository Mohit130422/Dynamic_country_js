    Yoga Itenary
    Yin Yoga

    Focus on physical & mental health. A combination of physical postures, breathing techniques & meditation to bring about a balance in body, mind, heart & soul.

    Vinayasa Yoga

    Traditional, highly active & dynamic form. Generates an internal heat that cleanses, purifies, creates a strong supple body, and brings clarity to the mind.

    Hatha Yoga

    Fast-paced & intense form. Builds strength, endurance, & burns calories. Emphasis on flow from one pose to the next rather than performing poses separately.

    Power Yoga

    Works deeply into body with passive longer held poses, targets deepest connective tissues inducing calmness and reducing stress and anxiety.
